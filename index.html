About citbif

Chennai Institute Technology Business Incubation Forum (CITBIF) is an incubation cell with a vision to create a sustainable ecosystem for nurturing creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship, particularly in the areas of technology interventions and agricultural productivity to support rural empowerment. CITBIF offers co-creation opportunities, access to co-working facilities, business support services, market connections, access to mentors and experts, funding facilitation, patents support, and access to industrial-grade prototyping lab and pilot production facilities. CITBIF has supported 27+ startups and enterprises and conducted 75+ skill-based training programs, including ideathons, innovators pitch league, call for faculty ideathon, farm to fork, and trendathon.

F.A.Q

  • What is the vision and the soul purpose of this event?

    The event emphasises on showcasing structured entrepreneurship on a global front and to build a resilient community between various startup ventures and businesses.

  • What is the agenda of the event?

    The event will take place in the college premises and will be presided by entrepreneurs and business enthusiasts from various fields of the industry , who will be sharing their experiences and journey with the fellow attendees.

  • Who are the speakers who are going to join us on the day of the event and which domain are they from?

    All speakers are from an industry background. They will be providing a strategical insight on the pros and cons of the modern industrial world which will be of great value for growing startups and business ventures.

  • What are the plans for future events, and how can attendees stay informed about upcoming opportunities?

    G20 is a global summit which takes place periodically and will continue to be so. Updates on the upcoming events will be provided via the G20 website.

  • What will be the main benefit for young generation entrepreneurs and startup idealists ?

    For growing startup idealists, this event is of immense importance as they would receive the guidance and support necessary to implement their ideas. They would also have a clearer view on the feasibility of their ideas at present.

  • What should I expect to learn or gain from attending the event?

    Startup20 provides valuable insights, networking opportunities, and resources for entrepreneurs and startups looking to build and grow their businesses, as well as inspiration and motivation to pursue business goals.
