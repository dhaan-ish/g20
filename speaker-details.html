Ms. Sushmita Kaneri

Social Entrepreneur | Creator at Gullak

Ms. Susmita Kaneri is a Software Engineer by qualification and a Social Entrepreneur by passion. Her interest in social work began at the age of 13 when she started working with communities and NGOs. This early experience gave her a unique perspective on the challenging situations that prevail in society. Her engineering background also helped her to understand how technology can be used to solve these challenges.


As a social entrepreneur, Ms. Kaneri founded Gullak, an NGO that aims to generate and enhance sustainable livelihoods for tribal and rural handicraft communities. Gullak provides support capital, methodologies, and access to markets, empowering these communities to become self-sufficient and financially independent. Through Gullak, Ms. Kaneri has been able to combine her passion for social work and her expertise in engineering to make a positive impact on society.
